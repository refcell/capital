## Key Assumptions And Extrapolation Notes

1. Revenue projections beyond FY27 are extrapolated. Visible public estimates from Yahoo Finance cover FY2026-FY2027 only. Years 3-10 use CAGR assumptions based on grocery industry growth trends and company-specific margin recovery trajectories.
2. FCF estimates are derived from revenue × assumed FCF margin. The current FCF margin is estimated at ~0.8-1.0% based on OCF minus capex ($2,367M - $1,679M = $688M). The Yahoo stats levered FCF of $1,530M is excluded from the conservative estimate.
3. Net debt of $8.34B is held constant in projections. In practice, the company may refinance some debt in 2027-2028, which could change the interest cost profile materially.
4. Share count assumptions use current diluted average of 547.2M. Treasury stock of 101.2M suggests potential for further share count decline through buybacks or recycling of treasury positions.
5. No tax rate adjustment. All scenarios use the effective tax rate of 18.8% implied by current TTM figures.
6. Interest expense held at $489M in early years, declining as debt is paid down. The cost of debt of 3.2% is used consistently.
