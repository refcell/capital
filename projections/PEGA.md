## Key Assumptions and Caveats

1. All projections use fiscal year alignment with Yahoo Finance consensus data (calendar year ending December).
2. EPS estimates are non-GAAP/normalized per Yahoo Finance data.
3. Share count assumed to decline through buybacks: 167.1M → 160M (base) → 155M (bull) by year 10.
4. Net cash position assumed constant at ~$402M, which is conservative — buybacks would reduce this and debt could be re-issued in the bull case.
5. Revenue growth extrapolated beyond FY27 public estimates for years 3-10. The 7%/11%/15% CAGR assumptions bridge from the FY25 peak of $1.746B to the terminal year.
6. Terminal multiples (14x/17x/21x FCF) are based on SaaS sector comparables adjusted for growth rate and quality.
7. The Q4 FY26 EPS miss of -33.6% is the most significant risk to all scenarios. If this proves structural rather than transient, all three cases need downward revision.
8. FCF margin assumptions do not account for potential investment acceleration (AI R&D, sales expansion) that could temporarily compress FCF before expansion.
